About GTAF

The Green Taxation and Accountability Framework (GTAF) integrates sustainability into Egypt's fiscal policies by offering tax incentives for businesses adopting green practices.

Through this innovative approach, we aim to transition Egypt to a nature-positive economy, ensuring long-term environmental and economic benefits for all sectors.

Services

  • Tax incentives for businesses investing in renewable energy
  • Support for SMEs and rural communities
  • Guidance on sustainable business practices
  • Environmental impact assessment and reporting
  • Green certification programs

Impact

By redirecting investments toward sustainable solutions, GTAF will reduce carbon emissions, protect biodiversity, and empower businesses to adopt environmentally responsible practices.

Our framework has already helped numerous organizations transition to greener practices while maintaining profitable operations.
