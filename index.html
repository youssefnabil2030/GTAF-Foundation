About GTAF Foundation

GTAF Foundation works to protect the environment through smart tax policies and honest government systems. We help create fair rules that support green projects, reduce pollution, and make sure public money is used wisely. Our goal is to build a cleaner, fairer future by combining environmental care with strong economic planning.
